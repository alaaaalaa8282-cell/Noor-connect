Noor Connect is a comprehensive, beautifully designed, and strictly privacy-first Islamic digital companion. Built to modern standards, it serves as your premium, tracker-free tool for daily spiritual growth.

Key Features & Tools:
• Enhanced Quran & Tafsir: Clean Uthmani/Indo-Pak interfaces with 25+ global Tafsir editions and multilingual translations instantly accessible offline.
• Premium Qibla Compass: High-precision native sensor integration with an exquisite UI, Kaaba alignment glow effects, and haptic feedback.
• Smart Prayer Times & Adhan: Fast, precise offline calculations (Hanafi & Shafi'i) with granular audio toggles for individual prayers and a robust background alarm engine.
• Adaptive Tracking & Menstrual Mode: Intelligent routing that adapts to your phase—automatically silencing obligatory Adhans and halting charts without breaking streaks.
• Essential Calculators: Built-in advanced tools to accurately determine annual Zakat obligations and Ramadan Fitrana.
• Extensive Islamic Library: Authentic Hisnul Muslim Duas, 99 Names of Allah with animations, gamified Islamic Quizzes, Tibb-e-Nabawi remedies, and complete Eid checklists.
• Qaza & Habit Ledgers: Long-term offline ledger for missed prayers (Qaza Tracker), digital Tasbeeh goals, and weekly spiritual habit consistency.
• Rich Media: 24/7 Live Makkah/Madinah broadcast streams and optimized, lag-free Hadith encyclopedias.

Privacy First Principle:
Noor Connect strictly enforces Zero Analytics and Zero Trackers. All personal data, charts, and habits are stored purely on your local device. The code is entirely open-source, giving you absolute transparency and peace of mind.
